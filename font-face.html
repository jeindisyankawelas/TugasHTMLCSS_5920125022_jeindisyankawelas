Briard

by ivy Duckett

The briard,or berger de brie, is a large breed of dog traditionally used as a herder and guardian of sheep.

Breed History

The briard,which is believed to have originated in france,has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep.The breed was used by the french Army asantries, messengers and to search for wounded soldiers because of its finesense of hearding. Briards were esed inthe first world war almost to the point of extincition currently. the population of briards is slowly recovering. Charlemagne, Napoleon, Thomas jefferson and lafayette all owned briards.
